# 📱 HOW TO GET YOUR APK — COMPLETE BEGINNER GUIDE

Follow these steps **exactly** and you'll have your APK in about 10 minutes.

---

## STEP 1 — Make a GitHub account

1. Open your browser and go to: **https://github.com**
2. Click the big **"Sign up"** button
3. Enter your email address → click **Continue**
4. Create a password → click **Continue**
5. Choose a username (example: `jamalaamirkhan`) → click **Continue**
6. Solve the puzzle, then check your email and click the verify link
7. When asked "How many team members?" → pick **"Just me"**
8. When asked plans → pick **"Free"** (bottom option)
9. ✅ You now have a GitHub account!

---

## STEP 2 — Create a new repository (project folder)

1. After logging in, look at the top-right corner — click the **"+"** icon
2. Click **"New repository"**
3. In the **"Repository name"** box type exactly: `hiit-timer`
4. Make sure **"Public"** is selected (the first option)
5. Scroll down, check the box next to **"Add a README file"**
6. Click the green **"Create repository"** button
7. ✅ Your project folder is created!

---

## STEP 3 — Upload all the files

1. You should now see your new repository page (it shows a README.md file)
2. Click **"Add file"** button (near the top)
3. Click **"Upload files"**
4. A page opens with a big dotted box that says "Drag files here"
5. **Open the ZIP file** I gave you (`hiit-timer-github.zip`) on your computer — extract/unzip it first
6. Select **ALL** the files and folders inside the unzipped folder
7. Drag them all into the dotted box on GitHub
8. Wait for them all to upload (you'll see a list of file names appear)
9. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
10. In the box that says "Add an optional extended description" — leave it blank
11. Click the green **"Commit changes"** button
12. ✅ All files are uploaded!

---

## STEP 4 — Watch it build automatically!

1. Click on the **"Actions"** tab at the top of your repository page
2. You'll see **"Build HIIT Timer APK"** with an orange/yellow circle ⏳ — that means it's building!
3. Click on it to watch the progress
4. Wait about **5-8 minutes** for it to finish
5. When you see a green checkmark ✅ — it's done!

---

## STEP 5 — Download your APK

1. Click on the green checkmark build (or click **"Build HIIT Timer APK"**)
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
3. You'll see a section called **"Artifacts"**
4. Click **"HIIT-Interval-Timer-APK"** to download it
5. It downloads as a ZIP — unzip it
6. Inside you'll find: **`app-debug.apk`** — that's your app! 🎉

---

## STEP 6 — Install on your Android phone

1. Copy the `app-debug.apk` file to your phone
   - You can email it to yourself, or
   - Send it via WhatsApp to yourself, or
   - Connect phone via USB and copy it
2. On your phone, find the APK file and tap it
3. Android will say **"Install blocked"** — tap **"Settings"**
4. Turn on **"Allow from this source"**
5. Go back and tap **Install**
6. Tap **Open** — your app is running! 🚀

---

## ❓ Problems?

- **Build failed (red X)?** → Click on it and look for the error message, send it to me
- **Can't find Artifacts?** → Make sure the build finished (green checkmark) first
- **Phone won't install?** → Make sure "Install unknown apps" is enabled in phone settings
