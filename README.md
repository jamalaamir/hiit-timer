## 📖 About The App

HIIT Interval Timer by Jamal Aamir Khan is a completely free workout timer app designed for anyone who takes their fitness seriously. Whether you are just starting out or you are a seasoned athlete, this app has everything you need to power through your workouts.

No ads. No subscriptions. No login. Just pure training.

---

## ✨ Features

### ⏱ Interval Timer
The heart of the app. Set your own Warm Up, Work and Rest durations and choose how many rounds you want. The timer guides you through every phase with color-coded visuals and distinct audio alerts so you always know exactly where you are in your workout — even without looking at the screen.

- Fully customizable Warm Up, Work and Rest intervals
- Set rounds from 1 to 30
- Color-coded phases — Orange for Warm Up, Green for Work, Blue for Rest
- Distinct audio alert for every phase change
- 5 second countdown before each phase begins
- Ready-made presets for Beginner, Athlete, Beast, Tabata, Boxing Round, Cardio Burn and Navy SEAL workouts
- Screen stays ON for your entire workout

### 🕐 Stopwatch
A precision stopwatch built right into the app. Record laps, track your splits and see your fastest and slowest laps highlighted automatically.

- HH:MM:SS precision timing
- Lap recording with individual split times
- Fastest lap and slowest lap highlighted automatically
- Full lap summary

### 🎵 Music Player
Load your own music from your device and let the app handle the rest. No streaming required — your music, your way.

- Load multiple songs at once from your device
- Shuffle or In Order playback modes
- Automatically plays the next song
- Seek bar, volume control and full playlist view

### 🎨 Themes & Sounds
Personalize the look and feel of the app to match your style. Choose from multiple themes and sound packs — all completely free.

- 6 themes — Light, Dark, Red Fire, Ocean, Purple Beast and Gold Champion
- 4 sound packs — Default, Boxing Bell, Military and Zen
- All themes and sounds are free with no ads or purchases required
